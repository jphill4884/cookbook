Ingredients
230g gram flour
830ml water
1 teaspoon salt
60ml olive oil

Instructions

In a large mixing bowl, pour in the flour. Add the water gradually as you whisk the flour to keep any lumps from forming. Once all the water has been added, mix until completely smooth and add salt and pepper as desired.
Let the mixture stand on the counter for 3 hours or so. When ready to bake, heat the oven to 175 degrees. Gently skim off any froth that forms on the surface of the mixture with a slotted spoon. Prepare a large rimmed cookie sheet by pouring the olive oil onto the bottom (if you can, do not be shy or skimpy here with the olive oil, it is wonderful in this recipe). Once the oven is hot, pour in the batter, making a layer about 1cm deep. Careful, this is going to move a lot when you pick it up! Bake at 175 for 30 minutes, until golden.
Remove from the oven when done and let cool a little before cutting.
